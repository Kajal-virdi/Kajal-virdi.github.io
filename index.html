Project3: De - Icing : Snow Cleaning App



We have plenty of snowfall in Canada every year, it's essential that snow is routinely removed from your roof, pathways and Car to prevent slipping/skidding, ice dams, water infiltration, and even roof collapse. Some people/organization find it very difficult to clear the snow due to lack of time, proper knowledge, equipment, manpower or their physical inability to do things. It is a landscaping service app and helps people to connect with the snow shovelers when they are in need of snow removal services. This app is the quickest way to get walk shovelled.It gives the power to the user to order a snow shoveler in a few clicks. Users can use it anywhere and at any time and get quick and transparent snow cleaning services within hours.
